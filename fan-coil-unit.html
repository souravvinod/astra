Single Skin or Double Skin?

The single skin FCU uses a GI chassis with direct-expansion or chilled water coil and a forward-curved centrifugal fan. Lower first cost and compact dimensions for residential and light commercial ceiling voids. Available in 2-pipe and 4-pipe configurations.

Cost-Effective Room Conditioning

Lower first cost than double skin. Ideal for residential apartments, budget hotels, and light commercial spaces where acoustic performance of the casing is not specified.

⚡

EC Fan Option

EC (electronically commutated) fan motor option delivers continuously variable speed control and 30–40% lower energy consumption vs standard 3-speed motors.

🌡️

2-Pipe & 4-Pipe

Both pipework configurations are available on the same chassis. 4-pipe units carry separate CHW and HHW circuits — enabling simultaneous heating and cooling across the building.

🔧

Easy Filter Access

Bottom-access G3 filter slides out without removing the ceiling grille. Monthly filter cleaning in under 5 minutes with no tools required.

The double skin FCU adds a PUF insulated outer casing over the GI chassis, with a thermally broken aluminium frame and factory-fitted flexible acoustic connection to the duct. Significantly reduces break-out noise from the fan and coil sections — the preferred specification for premium hotels, hospitals, and corporate offices where acoustic comfort is paramount.

Acoustic Performance — NC 25

PUF outer casing + EC fan + flexible acoustic duct collars achieve NC 25–30 at low speed in a standard hotel guest room. Meets most premium acoustic specifications.

⚡

EC Fan — Standard

Continuously variable EC fan comes standard on the double skin unit. Allows the BMS or room thermostat to modulate fan speed rather than switch between fixed speeds.

❄️

Insulated Drain Pan

304 SS drain pan with external PUF insulation prevents condensation on the pan exterior in humid climates — eliminating ceiling staining and mould risk.

🔗

BMS Ready

EC fan controller accepts 0–10V signal from the room thermostat or BMS. BACnet/Modbus gateway option for full integration into the building management system.
