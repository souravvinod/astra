Detailed Specifications

The single skin ventilation unit is a cost-effective supply or extract air mover with G4 filtration. 0.8mm galvanised steel construction. Suited for non-conditioned spaces, carparks, plant rooms, and general industrial ventilation where acoustic or thermal performance of the casing is not critical.

💰

Cost-Effective Construction

Standard GI construction keeps first cost low. Ideal for budget-constrained projects where casing thermal or acoustic performance is not a specification requirement.

🔧

Simple Maintenance

Hinged G4 filter door accessible without tools. Filter change in under 5 minutes. Fan and motor accessible from the same face.

⚡

Compact & Lightweight

Lighter than double skin equivalent — easier to handle and install in plant rooms, roof tops, and ceiling voids. Standard duct flanges on all connections.

🔄

Supply or Extract

Available configured for supply (fresh air in) or extract (stale air out). Specify direction and inlet/outlet positions at order stage.

🚗

Carparks

Supply and extract units for basement carpark ventilation. Standard GI is adequate for this non-conditioned environment.

🏭

Plant Rooms

General ventilation for electrical rooms, pump rooms, and UPS rooms where conditioned air is not required.

🏗️

Industrial Buildings

Supply air dilution ventilation in warehouses and light manufacturing where no thermal performance requirement exists.

🏊

Pool / Utility Areas

Extract units for swimming pool plant rooms and utility corridors where chemical resistance is not required.

🏢

Service Cores

Pressurisation and extract for stairwells, lift lobbies, and service risers where acoustic performance is not critical.

♻️

Toilet Extract

Common area and WC extract in office buildings where a simple filtered extract unit is all that is required.

The double skin ventilation unit uses a PUF sandwich panel construction — pre-coated GI inner and outer liners bonded to a PUF core — with a thermally broken aluminium extrusion frame. It delivers significantly better thermal and acoustic performance than the single skin equivalent, and shares the same frame system as the full AHU range.

🔇

Superior Acoustic Performance

PUF core sandwich construction reduces break-out noise by 8–12 dB compared to single skin at equivalent fan duty. 50mm option for noise-sensitive locations.

❄️

Thermal Break — No Condensation

Polyamide thermal break in the aluminium frame prevents cold bridging. No condensation on the casing exterior in air-conditioned plant rooms.

🔒

L2 Casing Leakage

Same casing leakage standard as the full AHU range. Minimises unfiltered air bypass and ensures the delivered airflow matches the design specification.

🔧

Full-Height Access

Hinged full-height access doors on all sections. Quarter-turn latches — no tools. Same serviceability as the Standard AHU.

🏢

Conditioned Offices

Fresh air supply units in conditioned office buildings where casing heat gain / loss affects delivered air temperature.

🏨

Hotels

Corridor supply and extract in acoustic-sensitive hotel environments. 50mm PUF option for fan break-out noise reduction.

🏥

Healthcare

Toilet extract and service shaft ventilation in hospitals where EN 1886 casing leakage is specified to prevent cross-contamination.

🎓

Schools

Classroom supply units with higher filtration grade (F7) where acoustic performance is required adjacent to teaching spaces.

🛒

Retail

Service area ventilation in shopping centres where consistent casing appearance and acoustic rating are required by the developer specification.

🏭

Pharma / Food

GMP area ventilation — double skin with L2 casing and F7–H13 filtration. Shares the same validated construction as the full AHU.
