Overview

Product Description

Astra Airtech's Air Handling Units are precision-engineered for a wide range of commercial HVAC applications. Available in horizontal and vertical configurations, our AHUs are manufactured using galvanised steel double-skin panels with thermal and acoustic insulation, ensuring high energy efficiency and low noise operation.

At the concept stage, our engineering team provides detailed inputs on critical parameters including air velocity, static pressure, fan selection, coil sizing, noise levels, and control sequences — ensuring the right product is selected for every application.

Product Overview

The Horizontal AHU with Heat Recovery Wheel (HRW) is designed for applications where energy recovery from exhaust air is critical. The rotary heat recovery wheel transfers both sensible and latent heat from exhaust air to incoming fresh air, achieving efficiency levels up to 85%.

Ideal for buildings with high fresh air requirements such as hospitals, labs, offices, and educational institutions where reducing HVAC energy consumption is a priority.

Product Overview

The Horizontal AHU with Plate Heat Exchanger (PHE) uses a fixed cross-flow aluminium plate heat exchanger to recover energy from exhaust air. With no moving parts in the heat exchanger, this system requires minimal maintenance while providing reliable and consistent energy savings.

Particularly suitable for applications requiring separation of supply and exhaust air streams such as hospitals, pharmaceuticals, and food processing facilities.

The Process

How We Manufacture

  • 01
    Design & Engineering

    Every AHU begins with an engineering review. Our team analyses airflow requirements, static pressure, coil sizing, fan selection, and panel configuration to produce detailed manufacturing drawings.

  • 02
    CNC Sheet Metal Fabrication

    Galvanised steel and aluminium sheets are cut, formed, and bent on CNC machines to tolerances of ±0.5mm, ensuring perfect fit and structural integrity of the AHU casing.

  • 03
    In-House Coil Manufacturing

    Cooling coils are manufactured on-site using fin-and-tube technology. Each coil is leak tested at 30 bar hydraulic pressure before installation in the AHU.

  • 04
    Panel Assembly & Insulation

    Double-skin panels are assembled with PU foam or mineral wool insulation. Internal galvanised steel surfaces are treated for corrosion resistance and air tightness.

  • 05
    Performance Testing

    Completed AHUs undergo airflow performance testing, static pressure testing, leakage testing to EN 1886 Class L2, and vibration analysis before factory dispatch.

  • 06
    Delivery & Site Support

    Our team supervises installation and commissioning on-site, and provides training to the client's maintenance team on operation and preventive maintenance procedures.
