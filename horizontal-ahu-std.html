Engineering Features

What's Built Into Every Unit

🔲

Thermal Break Frame

Aluminium extrusion with polyamide thermal break eliminates cold bridging and condensation on the casing exterior.

🌀

EC Fan Option

Electronically commutated plug fans offer 30–40% lower energy consumption vs standard forward-curved fans with VFD.

🔧

Full-Height Access

Hinged, full-height access panels on all service sections. Quarter-turn latches — no tools required. Complies with EN 13053.

💧

Double-Slope SS Drain Pan

304 stainless steel drain pan with double slope for complete drainage. Prevents standing water and bacterial growth (HACCP compliant).

🛡️

L2 Casing Leakage

Tested per EN 1886. Casing leakage class L2 ensures minimal energy loss and prevents bypass of filtered air.

⚙️

Modular Section Design

Each functional section (fan, filter, coil, mixing box) is independently removable. Future upgrades without full unit replacement.
