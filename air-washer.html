How It Works

Adiabatic Cooling Process — 5 Stages

01

Pre-Filter

G4 panel filter removes coarse dust from incoming airstream

02

Spray Banks

Rows of nozzles atomise water across full cross-section of duct

03

Adiabatic Cooling

Water evaporates — air temperature drops, humidity rises

04

Eliminators

Chevron or mesh eliminators capture unevaporated water droplets

05

Discharge

Cool, humid conditioned air delivered to supply ductwork

Engineering Features

Built for Industrial Process Environments

💧

Adiabatic Cooling — No Refrigerant

Pure water evaporation. No compressor, no refrigerant, no condenser. Operating cost is essentially just the pump and fan energy.

🎛️

Adjustable Saturation Efficiency

Efficiency is controlled by the number of active nozzle banks and water flow rate. Can be set from 70% to 95% saturation to match the humidity target.

🛡️

Low Carry-Over Eliminators

Chevron PVC eliminators capture droplets before they enter the supply duct. Carry-over tested at <0.003 g/m³ — preventing duct corrosion and condensation.

🏭

SS 304 / 316 Construction

Full stainless steel casing, sump, and nozzles available for food-grade and pharmaceutical applications where GI is not acceptable.

🔧

Easy Nozzle Maintenance

All nozzles are accessible from both sides without disconnecting pipework. Quick-release brass fittings allow individual nozzle removal in under 2 minutes.

💊

Water Treatment Ready

Biocide dosing port, bleed-off valve, and UV lamp provision are standard. Prevents Legionella risk when properly maintained per HTM 04-01.

Typical Applications

Process Industries That Depend on Air Washers

🧵

Textile Manufacturing

Yarn strength and loom efficiency depend on maintained humidity. Air washers are the standard solution for spinning, weaving, and knitting mills.

🖨️

Printing & Packaging

Paper and board absorb moisture unevenly in low RH environments, causing misregistration. Air washers maintain 50–65% RH for consistent print quality.

🍃

Tobacco Processing

Leaf conditioning and cigarette manufacture require precise humidity control at high airflow rates.

🏭

Light Assembly

Evaporative cooling in non-air-conditioned factories in hot-dry climates — up to 10°C temperature drop at low cost.

🧪

Chemical Storage

Humidity control in chemical warehouses to prevent hygroscopic product degradation.

🍎

Cold Storage Pre-Cooling

Evaporative pre-cooling of ambient air before mechanical refrigeration reduces compressor load in cold storage facilities.
