Selection Guide

Which AHU is Right for Your Project?

Use these application profiles to narrow your selection. Our engineering team can assist with final equipment sizing.

🏢

Standard Commercial Cooling

General office, retail, or institutional HVAC where chilled water or DX cooling is required without energy recovery or special treatment.

→ Recommended: AHU STD
🤖

Smart Building / LEED Projects

BMS-integrated developments where demand-controlled ventilation, VFD operation, and remote monitoring are required for energy certification.

→ Recommended: Smart AHU
🏥

High Fresh-Air Load (Hospitals, Labs)

Applications where 100% OA is required and energy cost of conditioning fresh air is significant. Both sensible and latent recovery needed.

→ Recommended: AHU with HRW
🧪

Critical Environments (Pharma, Food)

Processes where exhaust and supply air streams must remain completely separated. Zero cross-contamination and cleanroom compatibility required.

→ Recommended: AHU with PHE
🏭

Textile / Printing — Humidity Control

Process applications requiring simultaneous evaporative cooling and precise humidity addition. Single unit replaces separate humidifier and cooler.

→ Recommended: Air Washer
💨

Industrial Fume / Odour Removal

Paint shops, chemical plants, or food processing where particulate, odour, or VOC removal from process exhaust is required before discharge.

→ Recommended: Air Scrubber
