Wet Scrubber or Dry Scrubber?

The wet scrubber passes contaminated air through a water curtain (or packed bed with recirculated liquid), where soluble gases, acidic fumes, and particulates are absorbed and washed into the sump. Highly effective for chemical and industrial process exhaust.

Chemical Scrubbing

Recirculated caustic or acid solution neutralises specific contaminants. pH dosing system maintains scrubbing effectiveness automatically.

🏭

FRP Construction for Corrosives

Fibreglass reinforced polyester casing and fan for highly corrosive exhaust streams (HCl, H₂SO₄, HNO₃) where GI and SS are inadequate.

📊

pH Monitoring

Inline pH sensor and automatic dosing pump maintain scrubbing liquid chemistry within the optimal window for maximum removal efficiency.

🔧

Easy Packing Access

Top-access manway allows inspection and replacement of packing media without dismantling ductwork connections.

🧪

Chemical Plants

HCl, NH₃, SO₂, and chlorine fume scrubbing before exhaust discharge to atmosphere.

🏗️

Metal Pickling & Plating

Acid fume capture from pickling baths, anodising tanks, and electroplating lines.

💊

Pharma Synthesis

Solvent vapour and process exhaust scrubbing in API manufacturing and chemical synthesis.

🍕

Food Processing

Odour control from rendering, fish processing, or fermentation operations.

Waste Treatment

H₂S and ammonia scrubbing from sewage treatment plants and composting facilities.

The dry scrubber passes exhaust air through beds of filter media — activated carbon, zeolite, potassium permanganate, or specialty media — to adsorb or chemically react with odours, VOCs, and reactive gases. No water system required, making it simpler to install and maintain.

🌿

No Water System

Completely dry operation — no sump, no pump, no bleed-off. Simpler installation, no risk of Legionella, and no water treatment requirements.

🔧

Front-Loading Media Drawers

Media cassettes slide out from the front face — no tools, no ductwork disconnection. Media replacement takes under 30 minutes.

🎛️

Media Flexibility

Media type and bed depth are selected to the specific contaminant. Activated carbon for VOCs, KMnO₄ for H₂S and ethylene, zeolite for ammonia.

📏

Compact Footprint

No sump or water storage tank. Unit depth is significantly less than a wet scrubber of equivalent duty — suited for space-constrained plant rooms.

🏨

Hotel Kitchens

Activated carbon beds remove cooking odours from kitchen exhaust before discharge — where wet scrubbing is not feasible.

🏥

Hospital Mortuary

Formaldehyde and VOC removal from mortuary and pathology exhaust air streams.

🖥️

Data Centre Exhaust

Carbon beds remove ozone generated by UPS equipment and laser printers from server room exhaust.

🌸

Cosmetics Manufacturing

Fragrance VOC capture from production areas to protect ambient air quality and comply with emission limits.

🎨

Paint Spray Booths

Solvent vapour removal from spray booth exhaust. Carbon media selected to match specific solvent spectrum.

📦

Cold Storage

Ethylene removal from fruit and vegetable cold stores. KMnO₄ media oxidises ethylene to extend produce shelf life.
