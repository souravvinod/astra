Our Process

Built with Precision

From engineering brief to factory dispatch — every Astra Airtech unit follows a rigorous 4-stage manufacturing process.

01

Engineering

Selection calculations, shop drawings, GA drawings, and BOM — reviewed and signed off before fabrication begins.

02

Fabrication

CNC-cut double-skin GI panels with PUF insulation, aluminium thermal breaks, and powder-coated finish.

03

Assembly

Fan-motor sets, coil sections, filter banks, and controls assembled and aligned on calibrated fixtures.

04

Testing

Airflow performance, coil hydraulic test, motor run test, and acoustic checks — FAT report on delivery.
